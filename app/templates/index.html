About CellScope

The CellScope application is designed to simplify and automate the process of cell counting from microscopic imagery. Utilizing advanced algorithms, it allows users to quickly upload images and receive accurate cell counts, significantly reducing manual counting error and effort.

Continual improvements and updates are made to ensure the highest level of accuracy and user-friendliness. We welcome feedback and suggestions as we strive to make C-Count V1 the standard tool for cell counting in the scientific community.
