About CellScope

The CellScope application is designed to simplify and automate the process of cell counting from microscopic imagery. Utilizing advanced algorithms, it allows users to quickly upload images and receive accurate cell counts, significantly reducing manual counting error and effort.

Continual improvements and updates are made to ensure the highest level of accuracy and user-friendliness. We welcome feedback and suggestions as we strive to make CellScope or C-Count V1 the standard tool for cell counting in the scientific community.

Features

Advanced Cell Counting Algorithms: CellScope leverages state-of-the-art image processing algorithms to provide fast and accurate cell counts from microscopic images. This reduces manual counting errors and saves valuable time for researchers and laboratory technicians.
